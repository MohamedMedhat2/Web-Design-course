Football is a family of team sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball to score a goal. Unqualified, the word football normally means the form of football that is the most popular where the word is used. hand ball


Handball also known as team handball, European handball or Olympic handball is a team sport in which two teams of seven players each six outcourt players and a goalkeeperpass a ball using their hands with the aim of throwing it into the goal of the other team. A standard match consists of two periods of 30 minutes, and the team that scores more goals wins.
